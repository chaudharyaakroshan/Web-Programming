Top 5 Destinations

  • Kathmandu – The capital city of Nepal, rich in culture, temples, history, and traditional architecture.
  • Pokhara – Known for Phewa Lake, mountain views, adventure sports, and peaceful surroundings.
  • Mustang – Remote region famous for desert landscapes, caves, and Tibetan culture.
  • Janakpur – Birthplace of Goddess Sita and home to Janaki Temple.
  • Chitwan – Famous for Chitwan National Park, wildlife safaris, and biodiversity.
